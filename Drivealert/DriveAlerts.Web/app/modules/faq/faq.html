FAQ

What operating systems does DriveAlertNow on?

Android phones using version 4.3 and higher.

IOS or iPhones using software versions 7.1 and higher.

What is the Activation Module?

The Activation Module is a box that communicates and works in combination with the APP that is downloaded onto the driver’s phone (s).

The Activation Module is the small black box that should be attached, using the two-sided adhesive tape, on the windshield, dashboard, or sun visor, on the left side of the driver.

Be certain the placement does not impair your vision.

How many Activation Modules come with a subscription?

1 Activation Module per vehicle and up to 4 drivers or phones can connect to each Activation Module.

Do I have to use the Activation Module with the DriveAlertNow APP?

Yes. A DriveAlertNow APP must be downloaded and installed on the phone of every driver you wish to prevent the use of the phone while the vehicle is moving or wish to monitor.

Do I need an Activation Module for each vehicle I own?

Yes, if you do not want drivers in either vehicle to use their phone while driving.

Each Activation Module can connect up to 4 phones or drivers.

Do the Activation Modules work in all vehicles?

Yes.

Can DriveAlertNow work with my car’s Bluetooth system?

Yes. DriveAlertNow fully supports built-in and aftermarket hands-free Bluetooth devices.

To allow your driver to use their phone in a “Hands-Free” manner, log into your dashboard, go to “Settings” and insure “Allow Hands-Free Bluetooth” is enabled.

Hands-Free allows both incoming and outgoing calls using your Bluetooth device and not by handling the phone.

Do I have to pair the car’s Bluetooth or after-market Bluetooth device to my phone?

Yes. If you wish to use your phone in Hands-Free mode, you will need to pair your phone to that vehicle’s Bluetooth device.

Is the driver’s phone always blocked from use?

The driver’s phone will be blocked from use while the vehicle is in motion and approximately 7 seconds after the vehicle has stopped.

When in “Hands-Free” mode, the phone is still blocked while moving, but does not prevent the use of the phone using Hands-Free.

Can I send text messages and use maps in hands-free mode with DriveAlertNow?

If you are using an iPhone, you will need to enable “Hey Siri” in the phone’s settings.

The iPhone’s power code also needs to be plugged into power to use “Hey Siri” in a hands-free manner.

If you are using an Android phone, you will need the “Android Auto” App.

What if the driver needs to make a phone call in an emergency?

911 is always allowed through the DriveAlertNow system.

Simply select the emergency link and it will open a screen that displays 911 and up to 2 other emergency contacts.

To add these 2 other emergency contacts, log into your dashboard, select “Settings” and in the lower right part of that screen, you can add up to 2 numbers.

Does the Activation Module need to be charged?

When you receive your Activation Module in the mail, it should be fully charged.

The driver will receive a text message when the Activation Module’s battery falls to 30%, instructing the driver to charge it. It the driver fails to charge it and the battery level falls below 25%, the Administrator will see a violation message under that driver in the Dashboard.

To charge the Activation Module, plug the 3-foot charging cable that came with the Activation Module into the charging port on the face of the device and plug the other end into the vehicle’s USB port and charge for 6 hours.

What happens if the driver turns off the phone’s Bluetooth?

This is a violation. Driver’s using Android phones are unable to turn off Bluetooth.

iPhone drivers will see an alert on their screen to turn BT back on..

As soon as they turn Bluetooth off, it will be logged and timestamped in the Dashboard as a violation.

How do I set up driving parameters for my drivers?

Go to www.DriveAlertNow.com and in the upper right select “Login”.

Enter the email address for your username and the password you used when purchasing the product.

You should now be in your private Dashboard, Next, select “Settings”.

Some features are already preset which can be changed.

The “Help” links in each section will explain how is feature works.

When you have finished setting up the driving policy for your driver (s), be certain to press “save all”.

I made changes to the driving policy settings but don’t see the changes?

Making changes after the initial set up can take up to several hours for the changes to be live.

We recommend restarting that phone to make the policy changes appear sooner.

Why don’t I see a blocking screen on my iPhone?

The iPhone does not have a fixed blocking screen like the Android version. However, the iPhone defaults to the locking screen if the driver attempts to leave the lock scree.
